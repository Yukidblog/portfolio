We are a professional team
at TIS leading the company's web3 business.

web3 is a social transformation that brings a digital economy where we can exchange values with each other. We are living in a complex and uncertain era. With the arrival of a new era, everyone is exploring their own transformation in order to survive. There is no winner between a centralized society and a decentralized autonomous society, they will coexist. We support web3 business planning and development, including the integration between web1/web2 and web3.

For our long-established customers, the adoption of web3 is not easy.
With deep understanding of web3, we can realize your demands.

Many people understands that the web3 adoption is necessary but may be struggling with how to build and operate it.
We have been supporting the social infrastructure with web1/web2 IT technology and understand that it is not easy for our
long-established customers to adapt to web3. The point is “web3 technology is just a means”.
We think the most important thing is how we realize our customers' vision through the integration of web3.
Based on your needs, we provide comprehensive support from business planning and system development to
internal learning and education.

【Creating an integrated economic zone that seamlessly connects all social spaces, including web3/web2】

We will establish our new business base through collaborating with companies and local governments,
co-creation with web3 related partners/business strategic partners.

Our capabilities

Sustainable contributions to society with web3.

Through creating new businesses in four focus areas:
“Urban concentration and regional decline”, “Low-carbon/decarbonization”, “Financial inclusion”, and “Health concerns”,
we aim not only to solve social issues but eventually to achieve sustainable contributions to society through DAOs.
We also contribute to co-creation businesses with everyone who is working in the same focus areas.

Digital transformation for business.

We provide total support for those who want to use web3 to grow their businesses with the following services.

  • web3 business design

    Support for planning and developing web3-based business models.

  • Optimal blockchain selection and technical verification

    Proposing optimal blockchain technologies and advice according to our customer's needs.

  • Smart contract development and audit, NFT Issuance

    System development and support using fungible tokens and non-fungible tokens, smart contract audit.
    System development and support using blockchain technology.

  • Node operation, DAO development and management

    Providing support for blockchain node operation, DAO project planning, development and management.

  • web3 developer training and onboarding support

    Providing support for private study sessions, non-fungible and fungible token trials, employee education, ideathon/hackathon planning, etc.

We all challenge ourselves to improve our web3 capabilities.

At TIS, we not only participate in web3 study sessions, ideathons, and hackathons from management to employees
but also provide many opportunities to gain familiarity with web3 through hands-on use,
such as web3 trial sessions using fungible tokens and non-fungible tokens,
digital badges for proof of participation in training and events, etc.
We aim to raise the knowledge level of our entire organization, transform our own company,
and continue to challenge ourselves to grow as individuals.

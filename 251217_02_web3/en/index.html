Society oriented, operationally diverse, globally active

We collaborate with local governments to build a new economic zone that connects society through the power of web3/Web2.0, guiding your business to a new stage.

TIS is a Professional in the web3 Business Field.

web3 technology enables a token economy where value can be exchanged among users. In order to succeed in web3 business within the token economy, it is crucial to understand ‘how to build and operate’ effectively. TIS, which has supported social infrastructure since the Web2.0 era, provides comprehensive support from planning to system development and education in the web3 business, where Web2.0 and web3 systems merge.

New Business Opportunities in the Token Economy Made Possible by web3.

The web3 token economy will promote the diversification of social connections and means of value exchange, and greatly improve convenience in various areas such as "People", "Goods", "Money", and "Information".
In such a web3 society, decentralized systems will enhance the transparency and reliability of data and enable efficient transactions. This is expected to lead to the visualization of consumer behavior and self-management of personal data, and the creation of new business models that will play a role in decentralized finance, digital identity, and supply chain transparency.
TIS will serve a multitude of roles in the token economy based on web3 technology, including the integration of Web2.0 and web3, and will support newly created businesses and municipal projects with a variety of services.

  • ❶ web3 Consulting

    We provide comprehensive support for business planning, web3 technology evaluation, and PoC for the creation of new services using web3 technology. In addition, based on the IT service knowledge that TIS has developed, we also provide support for issues related to the use of web3 technology, such as web3 onboarding support, training of client engineers, establishment of secure development systems with offshore blockchain companies, security diagnosis and smart contract code review.

  • ❷ web3 Service

    We offer services using web3 technology and provide white labels and components to help customers implement web3 services. We can provide a wide range of support, including providing components needed for tokenization of physical assets (RWA), implementing and operating decentralized autonomous organizations (DAO), and implementing payment systems for crypto assets and stablecoin. In this way, we aim to build services in a shorter period of time than customers would be able to promote independence.

  • ❸ web3 System Development and Node Operation Support

    We provide solutions for crypto wallet development, NFT issuance/ticketing/marketplace, crypto asset and stablecoin payments, and blockchain data analytics. Our support is wide as from development using white labels and components to requirement definition, development, migration, operation and maintenance of customers' own construction. We also support node operations of customers' original chains, leveraging our knowledge cultivated through blockchain node operations in co-creation companies.

  • ❹ Co-creation Innovative Services

    In the new Web2.0 and web3 merged market, we provide services to solve social issues by combining TIS' technological capabilities with the expertise of web3 co-creation partners. We also emphasize co-creation with strategic partners to expand the market through collaboration with customer channels. We are always looking for partners with innovative web3 technologies and strategic partners to aggressively expand the web3 market, and we will contribute to the web3 industry through the creation of services through co-creation.

Service

Token-Based Branding Support Services

Token-Based Branding Support Services supports a series of steps to communicate the appeal of your products and experiences more widely through tokenization. The service accompanies manufacturers through the entire process, from planning conception and technical support to advice on legal compliance.

Tokenization of products and experiences improves liquidity by removing restrictions on physical distribution, as well as transparency as transactions are made on the blockchain. It is also a growing market, with a market size estimated to be worth around 2,00 trillion yen by 2030.

Made in Japan products and experiences that are painstakingly created with strong aspirations as the source are truly the pride of Japan. We believe that the value of these products and experiences can be more widely and appropriately delivered.

Let's leverage TIS's Token-Based Branding Support Services to make your unique products and experiences even more appealing to the world.

Value

The Value We Offer

New Logistics Through the Tokenization of Goods

Tokenizing products and experiences expands the sales market and also enables trading between individuals (secondary distribution), which improves liquidity and attracts new customers.

In addition, tokens issued on the blockchain are difficult to counterfeit or tamper with, and a high level of transparency is ensured as the transaction history is recorded from the issuer to the owner. This contributes to the enhancement of brand value as it ensures the delivery of authenticity.

Enables ongoing relationship building with consumers

Issuing tokens over the lifecycle of the product enables the creation of the ongoing manufacturer-consumer relationship.
For example, issuing Subscription token could be a way for consumers to empathies with the background of a pre-launch product and purchase it with the feeling of support. This purchasing method is also unique in that it allows for future price increases. Another possible mechanism is to issue post-purchase human fan certification tokens, which can then be used as tickets to attend fan events.

Token-based Experience Design

The service does not just provide the ability to tokenize and sell products. We design consumer experiences that maximize the value of the product, and are closely aligned with the wishes of the manufacturer.
